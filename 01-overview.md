# 1. Overview of the regimes

A quick orientation before the detail. All three are FATF-aligned, so the AML
*backbone* is portable; what differs is the licence model, capital, custody
philosophy and the regulator's temperament.

| Dimension | VARA (Dubai) | SFC (Hong Kong) | AFSA / AIFC (Astana) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Regulator | Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority | Securities & Futures Commission | Astana Financial Services Authority |
| Legal basis | Dubai Law No. 4 of 2022 + VARA Rulebooks | AMLO (Cap. 615) VASP regime + SFO for security tokens | AIFC Acts + AFSA Digital Asset framework |
| Licence model | Activity-based (7 activities) | VATP licence; dual regime with SFO | Digital Asset Trading Facility + related |
| Key roles | Compliance Officer + MLRO (pre-approved, resident) | ≥2 Responsible Officers; MLRO under AMLO | SEO, Finance Officer, Compliance Officer, MLRO |
| Min. capital (indicative) | Activity-dependent | Paid-up HK$5m + liquid ≥ HK$3m | Higher of USD 200k or 12 months opex |
| Custody | Separately licensed; segregation rules | Client assets via wholly-owned subsidiary; cold storage | Custody as a regulated activity |
| Travel Rule | Yes (FATF) | Yes | FATF-aligned |

> Figures are indicative and change — confirm against the current rulebooks.

## How to choose
Pick the jurisdiction by **target market, capital available, custody model and
token scope** — not by licence cost. A few rules of thumb:

- Serving the **Gulf / MENA** market or want an activity-by-activity licence → **VARA**.
- Need credibility with **institutional / APAC** clients, can meet strict custody
  and investor-protection bars → **SFC**.
- Want a **common-law hub** with lower capital entry and a fast-growing market,
  or a base for Central Asia → **AIFC**.

## The universal truth
Whichever you choose, the regulator examines the same things: a credible
business plan, fit-and-proper people, real capital, segregated custody, and an
**AML programme that works on day one** — not a policy PDF. The rest of this
playbook is about building that.
