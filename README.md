# VASP Licensing Playbook

A practitioner's playbook for taking a Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP)
through licensing across major regimes — **VARA (Dubai), SFC (Hong Kong) and
AIFC/AFSA (Astana)** — written from hands-on AML/compliance experience.

It turns "we want a licence" into a structured, examiner-defensible programme:
what to prepare, in what order, who you need, and how to answer the regulator.

> ⚠️ **General methodology only — not legal advice, and no confidential or
> employer data.** Every checklist, template and example here is built from
> public regulatory frameworks and professional practice. Always verify against
> the current official rulebooks and take qualified legal advice for your case.
